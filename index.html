The Problem

Wildfire smoke is a silent killer. Hazardous air quality affects millions of people, yet current tools only show data for right now. There is no reliable "weather forecast" for smoke 24 hours in advance.

The Solution

SmokeSignal uses a physics-aware AI approach. It fuses real-time satellite imagery with ground sensor networks to calculate pollution velocity and predict hazardous air quality events before they arrive.

Key Features

Physics-Aware AI

Calculates the physical velocity of smoke plumes to detect rapid wildfire spread instantly.

XAI Explainability

Uses SHAP values to explain why a prediction was made, building trust with users.

Dual-Layer Data

Combines satellite inputs with ground sensors for 99.5% accuracy in flagging hazardous days.
